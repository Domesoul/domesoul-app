Feels Like Heaven

By Domé Soul

Behind the Song

This song speaks of the sacred beauty found in love, faith, and divine presence. Written from a place of peace and connection, “Feels Like Heaven” invites listeners to remember the soft moments that make life radiant. It was inspired by personal encounters and a yearning for joy that transcends circumstances.
